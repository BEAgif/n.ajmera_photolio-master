Scientist, Student, Humanitarian

Welcome to my website. I'm a photographer who captures the temporary truths of life. Peruse my work, buy a print, or contact me for event work.

Nirvi Ajmera


By day I am a student at the University of Minnesota, majoring in psychology with minors in neuroscience and child psychology. By night I am a photographer, capturing the temporary truths of the world in snapshots. I enjoy photographing nature, humans, humans in nature, and the intersection of organic and artificial. No matter the subject, there is always a compelling shot waiting to be taken. Let me find yours.
